Free Angular 2 talk

September 22nd
6:00 PM

Detroit Labs
1520 Woodward Ave #600, Detroit, MI

"Angular 2 in Action" by Jaime Niswonger and Keyhole Software. They're touring this talk all the way from Kansas. The talk is FREE but you have to register to get a ticket. Here's some of the description:

In this talk, Keyhole Principal Consultant Jaime Niswonger will present a live demonstration of Angular 2 in action.

Throughout the talk, Jaime Niswonger will build an example Angular 2 application applicable to enterprise use. He will point out unique features and approaches the Angular 2 framework introduces, with an eye for potential pitfalls. The presentation will show how it contrasts from Angular 1 and other comparable JavaScript frameworks, in addition to a contrast from existing development patterns and techniques.

The presentation will be followed with a Q&A session, where Niswonger will answer any questions the audience has regarding Angular 2.

Food and drinks will be provided.

Meetup Changes

Starting with Meetup #6, the cost of Detroit JS Meetups is going up from free to $5. The primary reason for this change is to get people who RSVP to attend meetups to come to them. $5 is the minimum allowed by Meetup.com. Where will this money go? $15 of it will go to pay the Meetup.com costs, the rest will go to a local coding/computer science type charity. Which charity? I DON'T KNOW! Let's figure this out at our next meetup. Bring your thinking caps (that you wear with the brim in the back because you're so cool and edgy).
